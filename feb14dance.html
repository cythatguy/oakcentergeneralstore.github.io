Presents:

Square/Contra Dance

Bay City WI Town Hall

Saturday, February 14 @ 7:30pm

overview

Although not held at the Oak Center General Store, we have organized another square dance to be held at the Bay City Town Hall on Valentine’s Day! With a live band and caller, no experience is necessary to come to this dance. In days gone by, rural dances were where you'd go to mingle with potential suitors. What better day to continue this tradition than the fourteenth of February?! $10 cover and a dish to share!
